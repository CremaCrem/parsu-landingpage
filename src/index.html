Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Vision

A university of science and technology advancing geology and bio-resource science to promote sustainable and resilient communities.

Mission

To provide a high-quality, global educational experience to educate individuals in leadership, innovation, critical thinking, cultural appreciation and civic responsibility so that graduates are competent, informed and environmentally and culturally proactive and productive citizens.

Core Values

  • P - Passion for Excellence: We always aspire for competitiveness, creativity, and innovativeness.
  • S - Service: We care for marginalized communities and contribute our talents for promotion of sustainable ecosystems and their ecological services. We work hard and are tireless in the pursuit of our goals and achieving outcomes of the highest quality.
  • U - Unity and Inclusiveness: We are in solidarity with local and international communities, align our intent and actions consistent with the aspiration of the world for sustainable and resilient communities. We treat others with differential regard, honor each individual and celebrate differences.
